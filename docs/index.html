Captions that write themselves.

Word-by-word captions transcribed from the clip's audio by Whisper, running entirely on your machine — no upload, no API key, no per-minute fee. Restyle them, nudge the timing, or leave them as they land.

What it does

  • Face tracking

    Keeps the streamer centered as the shot reframes to fill a vertical frame.

  • One-paste import

    Paste a Twitch clip or VOD link. It downloads and loads straight into the timeline.

  • Preview matches export

    What you see is what renders. The live preview and the ffmpeg output are the same.

  • Project autosave

    Work is saved as you go. Reopen a project exactly where you left off.

  • Quiet updates

    New versions download in the background. One click to relaunch, no installers to chase.

Your clips never leave your computer.

No account, no telemetry, no cloud. Captions are transcribed offline and video is rendered locally — the app is open source, so you can verify every word of it.
